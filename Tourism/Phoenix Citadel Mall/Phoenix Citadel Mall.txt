Phoenix Citadel Mall

Phoenix Citadel Mall is a shopping mall in the city of Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India. It is owned by Phoenix Mills Ltd, they operate 10 malls across 7 cities of India. The mall is spread over 3 floors and has a dedicated building for parking. It has 1,000,000 leasing area for shopping brands. Phoenix Citadel Mall is considered the number one mall in Indore for a number of reasons. These include: Size and variety of stores: The mall has over 300 stores, including a wide variety of national and international brands
