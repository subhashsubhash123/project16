Sarafa Bazar

Sarafa Bazaar is a jewellery market and night street food court located in central Indore, India. Sarafa is one of the market in India which remains as a jewellery marketplace at daytime and converts itself into a street food court at night.Divided between Chota and Bada Sarafa Bazaar, the market is lined with shops selling jewellery. From antique to modern designs, at Sarafa you will be spoilt for choice between the most exquisite meenakari designs in the country. You will also find a wide variety of brass ornaments that are unique to the area.
