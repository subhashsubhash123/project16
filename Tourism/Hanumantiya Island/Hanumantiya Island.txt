Hanumantiya Island

The Hanuwantiya tapu is globally recognised for being the largest water and adventure vacation destination in IndiaNearest railway station is Khandwa station but popular option is Indore railway station due to good connectivity and best available taxi options. Distance from Khandwa railway station to Hanumantiya Island is just approx. 50 kms where as Indore railway station is 150 kms.
