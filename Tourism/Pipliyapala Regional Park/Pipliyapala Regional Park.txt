Pipliyapala Regional Park

It was named in honor of a former Prime Minister of India, Atal Bihari Vajpayee, as a tribute to his contribution to the nation. Since its inception, Pipliyapala Regional Park has become a cherished spot for locals and tourists alike.The park was developed and created by Indore Development Authority and was opened in 2003. Park is located on 80 acres of land with 42 acres for lake and other 38 for other attractions. Park is named after former Prime Minister of India Atal Bihari Vajpayee.
