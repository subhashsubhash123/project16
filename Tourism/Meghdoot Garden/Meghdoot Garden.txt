Meghdoot Garden

Popular spot for running, yoga & cricket amid greenery, plus camel rides & roaming peacocks.This park is located in vijay nagar and is well connected with all the local buses, riksha and cabs. The entry fee is only Rs. 5 in weekdays and Rs. 10 in weekends.Spread over 42 acres, the garden perfectly blends natural beauty, modern amenities, and recreational facilities, making it a favourite among locals and tourists. The garden was developed by the Indore Development Authority in the year 2000 and is named after the famous Sanskrit poet Kalidasa's work, Meghdoot.

Main Gate, Entrance, Maguda Nagar, Scheme No 54, Indore, Madhya Pradesh 452011
