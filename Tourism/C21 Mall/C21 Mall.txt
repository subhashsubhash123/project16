C21 Mall

Vast mall with global & national brands, a food court, a disco-pub, a multiplex & kids' play areas.The company CTPL, part of the Indore-based C-21 group, is promoted by Mr. Gurjeet Singh Chhabra and Mrs. Prabjot Kaur Chhabra. It owns and operates the 'C21 Mall' in Indore, which was opened to public in 2010 and is built over a total area of 5.25 lakh sq.
