Holkar Cricket Stadium

Holkar Cricket Stadium is located in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India. It is owned and operated by Madhya Pradesh Cricket Association and serves as its headquarter. It is the home ground of Madhya Pradesh cricket team, MP women's team.Indore's Holkar Cricket Stadium provides a batting-friendly surface in T20 cricket. The average first innings score here is 209 in three T20Is played here, which includes India's famous 260 total against Sri Lanka in 2017.
