Indore Zoo

You will see many beautiful birds and animals in Indore zoo . Secondly, it is very well maintained and it is one of the oldest zoological park in Madhya Pradesh spread over an area of 52 acres . The zoo has different varieties of animals and birds which have been brought from different parts of world.Fundamentally the establishment of Zoo is aimed at ex-situ conservation breeding, education and research. Indore zoo is currently housing 56 indigenous and exotic species numbering around 587 specimens.
